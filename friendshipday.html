Friendship Day

Events

A test of your balancing ability and footballing prowess. Spin your body ten times, kick the football and aim at the set targets. Each of these targets grab you a different score. Each team will consist of five members.

Dizzy Football

An obstacle relay race around college where each team member has to perform tasks one at a time to proceed to the next member and ultimately reach the finish line. A total of four tasks have to be completed by the four team members. Team which finishes with the best timing wins.

Need For Speed

Remember Tic Tac Toe, the best way to relax when you were a last bencher. The same old rules apply here, arrange the t-shirts in the right sequence and win the game. Oh! But the twist lies here, you need to run and capture your place where you want to make your mark. Also you can continue to change the position of your t-shirt in order to win. So show some pace and thinking in this team game.

Tic Tac Toe

5 members in a team. Dip the sponge in a bucket of water and pass it to your team members from above your head. The last one in line then squeezes the water out of the sponge into a bottle. Reach the brim of the bottle first and be declared as winner.

Fill Your Bottle

Shake every part of your body to get the ping pong balls out of the box tied to your waist. This simple yet fascinating game involves 3 players, player 1 needs to get the balls out of the box, player 2 needs to collect it and fill the box attached to player 3. Player 3 needs to shake and get the balls out again asap. Finally player 2 needs to collect balls and reach the finish line!

Shake That

Prove the unity of your class by forming a human chain. The class with the longest chain gets crowned the winner
