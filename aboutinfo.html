About FRCRCE Student Council

We represents the entire student population of the campus, embraces the vision, mission and strategic plan of the FRCRCE. We upholds the values of the FRCRCE students, namely integrity, commitment, accountability and respect. We serves the student's interests that are in line with the vision and mission of the college and that will not offend anyone who is a campus student and a member of management. We also acts as mediator between management and students and protect the image of the College.

Mission

  • To enhance the advancement of student life, quality education and student participation.

Vision

  • To contribute to maintaining and enhancing student life in all the relevant aspects such as academic and social areas.
  • To contribute to maintaining and enhancing student life in all the relevant aspects such as academic and social areas.
  • To mediate the gap between the students and management.
  • To improve transparency through consistent communication with the students.
  • To encourage and support active participation of all students in community engagement projects.
