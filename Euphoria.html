Euphoria '17

Events

Performing Arts

1. Do Re Mi: For all the budding Adeles and Enriques out there! Perform your favourite English songs and liven the hills with the sound of your music!!

2. Sa Re Ga Ma: This event is our attempt to discover the next Lata Mangeshkar or Kishore Kumar amongst us.

3. So You Think You Can Dance (Solo + Duet): (so you think you can dance?) If you do, this is the perfect opportunity for you to show us what you’ve got!

4. Dance Face Off: Showcase your dancing skills as you battle it out on the dance floor!

5. Karaoke: Getaway from the monochromatic life by relaxing with your friends while you sing out your all-time favourite songs.

6. CRCE’s Got Talent: What’s your X-factor?

Gaming Events

1. Lan Gaming : All those hours playing video games , did you ever think it would be put to use? Switch your gamer mode on and show us who’s the best.

2. Glow in the dark Cricket : What’s the same as box cricket, but better? Glow in the dark cricket!

3. Darts : Think you’ve Got Good aim? Here’s your chance to test it at this fun game of darts!

4. Foosball: Show us if you still got it in you if you’re tied up, as you look to score and save your goal in this human sized version of foosball.

5. Aba Dhubi : We’ve all played this game growing up, bring out the child in you as you play this game once more!

6. Real Chess: Would you have survived had you been in place of Harry and Ron playing wizard’s chess? Come find out in the muggle version of the game.

Fine Arts

1. Face Painting: Put a fellow mate behind a mask while showcasing your painting skills.

2. Rangoli: One of the most ancient traditions of our country…show us your version of it!

3. Nail Art: All you fashionistas out there put your best nails forward.

4. Color Splash : Escape the monochrome, bring the black and white image provided, to life, with your vivid imagination and coloring details!

5. Tatoo Making: Traditional, realism, tribal, Japanese…what’s your tattoo style?

Personality Events

1. Mr. and Ms. CRCE: This one is for all the BEs. No one ever said you have to be extra ordinary…but what’s the fun in being ordinary anyway? Do you think you are awesome enough to be crowned Mr./Ms. CRCE?


2. CRCE’s Fittest: Complete the given fitness tasks the quickest and be named CRCE’s Fittest.


3. Arm Wrestling: Aa Dekhe Zara, Kisme Kitna Hai Dum!


4. CRCE Roadies: Your wit, endurance and morale will be put to test here in CRCE Roadies. Disclaimer: Not for the faint hearted.


Literary Events

1. Extempore : How fast can you organize your thoughts? Here’s your chance to showcase your impromptu speech talent.

2. Creative Writing : Ever wondered what would have happened if Cindrella hadn’t gone to the ball? Or if Katniss had never volunteered? We give you a chance to rewrite famous stories according to your will!

3. Spell Bee : How well do you think you know the English language? Find out here as you are made to spell out a variety of words.

4. Just a Minute : How difficult is it to talk continuously for a minute without error? Here’s your chance to find out!

5. Fake a News : Not your ordinary news article! We’ll give you a headline and you have to come up with the most bizzare news piece for it. Let your imagination run wild!

6. Scrabble : Rest assured this game will keep your brain busy!

7. Parlimentary Debate : Captivated by Arnab Goswami? We give you a platform to showcase your debating skills here at CRCE.

8. Poetry Re-engineered : We bet you thought kindergarten was the last time you had to recite poetry! Enthrall the audience as you recite these poems in the most fascinating way possible.

Fun Events

1. Taboo : The most fun game ever played! Get your squad and play taboo where you have to make your team-mates guess maximum words without using the Taboo words. All of your squad’s inside jokes will come in handy and you’re gonna have a blast!

2. Ad Mad : Show us your acting and convincing skills as you make an advertisement for this bizzare and unusual product given by us!

3. MUnopoly : Engineering life is truly a roller coaster ride. We challenge you to this board game where in you are the tokens and Mumbai University is the board. Beware of KTs!

4. Charades : Enact these movies/ songs/ actions for your team-mates to guess!

5. Treasure Hunt: Awaken your inner Indiana Jones and hunt for the treasure awaiting you.

6. Poker : A classic game of poker. We warn you, stakes are high in this game of gambling.

7. Antakshari : For all those Bollywood song lovers out there. This one’s for you!

8. Quiz Up : Test your knowledge in these fun genres as you play this game!

9. Hangman : Please don’t let the poor man hang, save him by guessing the correct movie or song.
