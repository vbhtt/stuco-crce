Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  1. One speaker of the team makes the opening speech while the other makes the closing speech.

  2. The proposition starts the debate; the proposition gives the first opening speech, followed by the opposition’s opening speech.

  3. The opposition raises the first point in the refutation round.

  4. The last thirty seconds of Refutation Round at any stage of the competition is reserved for ‘The Concluding Statement’. It is a short concise and precise statement that sums up a team’s argument.

  5. The debate is concluded by the closing speech, first from the opposition and then the proposition.

  6. No new points may be introduced in the closing speech. If a certain team does bring up a new point; the opposition may request the moderator an opportunity to point out the new points at the end of the closing speech which will lead to negative marking.

  7. The topics will be given to the teams 15 minutes prior to their debate [1] . In these 15 minutes they are strictly prohibited to use the internet or any other reading material. They will be provided with papers and writing material to note-down their points which they can carry with themselves into the debate.

  8. Personal comments of any kind directed against the opposite team are strictly prohibited.

  9. Participants cannot comment on the opposite team’s stance or statements made in previous debates of the competition.

  10. Negative marking for exceeding time limit.

  11. Participants will be judged on the following criteria: Analysis, confidence, engagement with opposition, organization of points, argumentation level and rapport established with partner.

  12. Discrepancies of any sort during the debate will be resolved at the moderator’s discretion. The decision will be final and binding.

  13. Any electronic devices will not be allowed during the debate.

NOTE :
[1.] The topic for the first round of debate (League Round 1) will be given one week prior to the debate.
In the next three rounds (League Round 2 & 3 and the Quarter Finals), the topics will be given 15 minutes prior to the debate.
For the Semi-finals and the Finals the topics will be given one day in advance.

Time Stipulation

League & Quaters

  1. 4 minutes opening and closing speech.

  2. Warning bell after 3 minutes 45 seconds (1 bell).

  3. No new points can be introduced after the warning bell for the opening speaker. A contestant can however complete his current point and summarize.

  4. 2 bells after 4 minutes are completed.

  5. Contestants are expected to conclude in the next 15 seconds. He/she would not be allowed to speak after 4 min 15 seconds.

  6. The time for the refutation rounds is 10 minutes.

Semi-finals & Finals

  1. 5 minutes opening and closing speech.

  2. Warning bell after 4 minutes 45 seconds (1 bell).

  3. No new points can be introduced after the warning bell for the opening speaker. A contestant can however complete his current point and summarize.

  4. 2 bells after 5 minutes are completed.

  5. Contestants are expected to conclude in the next 15 seconds. He/she would not be allowed to speak after 5 min 15 seconds.

  6. The time for the refutation rounds is 15 minutes.

Format

    The deabate comprises of 4 Rounds :

  1. 3 League Rounds

  2. Quater Finals

  3. Semi Finals

  4. Finals

Structure of Rounds

League Rounds
Opening proposition speaker 4 minutes
Opening opposition speaker 4 minutes
Refutation Round 10 minutes
Concluding proposition speaker 4 minutes
Concluding opposition speaker 4 minutes
Quater Finals
Opening proposition speaker 4 minutes
Opening opposition speaker 4 minutes
Refutation Round 10 minutes
Concluding proposition speaker 4 minutes
Concluding opposition speaker 4 minutes
Semi Finals
Opening proposition speaker 5 minutes
Opening opposition speaker 5 minutes
Refutation Round 15 minutes
Concluding proposition speaker 5 minutes
Concluding opposition speaker 5 minutes
Finals
Opening proposition speaker 5 minutes
Opening opposition speaker 5 minutes
Refutation Round 20 minutes
Concluding proposition speaker 5 minutes
Concluding opposition speaker 5 minutes
